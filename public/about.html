Dare create tomorrow's economy!


Mornova is a dynamic and innovative startup based in Morocco, dedicated to revolutionizing the Moroccan startup ecosystem. We are driven by a mission to connect investors, entrepreneurs, and innovators, fostering the creation of startups and companies that drive economic growth and push the boundaries of innovation in Morocco.

At Mornova, our vision is to build a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem that fuels the growth of startups and empowers entrepreneurs to turn their ideas into successful businesses. We aim to create a supportive and collaborative environment where aspiring entrepreneurs can connect with experienced investors, access valuable resources, and find the support they need to transform their visions into reality.

Central to our mission is our comprehensive social networking platform, designed to be the go-to hub for all things startup-related in Morocco. Our platform provides a space for entrepreneurs to connect, collaborate, and share knowledge, enabling them to leverage the collective wisdom and experience of the community. Investors can discover promising startups, engage with founders, and explore investment opportunities tailored to their interests.
